WORD SEARCH

Puzzle,Logic

  • Technology: Unity
  • Platform: Windows,MacOS

This demo is a replica of the famous Word Search game which I initially made for a client.

The demo took me 4 days of part time work and required me to play the original word search in order to understand and build the logic from scratch.

Placing the words on a 2d grid while maintaining intersecting words was the most challenging part of the demo. The "word to grid placement" algorithm I built, ended up doing the job but given the time frame, I could only achieve horizontal and vertical placement but not diagonal placement. Featuring in diagonal placement with intersecting words would end up adding a layer of complexity to the logic that was not feasible given the time frame. Nonetheless, I am happy with how it turned out.
