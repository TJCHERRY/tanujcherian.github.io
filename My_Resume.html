About Me

Born and raised in Hyderabad, I started gaming at the age of 8. Being is the sphere of Game Development, I surprisingly don't play games on a daily basis. I do however, enjoy watching youtubers deconstruct games and their underlying systems. When I find myself playing games, I do with the intention of analyzing, deconstructing underlying gameplay mechanics,picking out subtle gameplay and design related nuances and absorbing its background scores.
