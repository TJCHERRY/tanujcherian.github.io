Introduction

Game Jams to many including me, is a sport. Participating in a game jam requires you to understand your strengths and weaknesses, and build a game with a specific theme, within a specific time period(usually 48 hours). The thrill of entering in a game jam and working with a team is awesome since it forces you to get your creative juices running and requires meticulous time management. I believe game jams are very important as they become a hub for creative ideas and games. Two of the biggest indie games in the market: Hollow Knight and Celeste, were the products of game jams.

This June onward, I decided to spend a weekend taking part in one game jame every month.Because you never know what you might end up making 😃.
