Mastermind

Puzzle,Logic

  • Technology: Unity
  • Platform: Windows

This is a student project which is a replica of the popular puzzle board game MasterMind

Instructions

The physical game requires 2 players:

  • The hider who hides a combination of 6 colours of any permutation.
  • The seeker who is required to guess this permutation of colours within a finite amount of attempts(No. of attempts may vary but standard is 10).
    • The hider’s role does not tend there.
  • The hider is supposed provide clues after every attempt made by the seeker.
  • The hider is meant to analyze the clues and make changes during his/her next try in order to bring him/her closer to the hidden combination.
  • Failure to seek the correct combination within the given number of attempts results in the seeker’s loss and the hider’s win.
  • Successfully figuring out the combination within the given number of chances results in the seeker’s win and the hider’s loss.

Core Gameplay Loop

  • In this version of the game, the player is the seeker by default and has to figure out the hidden colour combination.
  • The A.I in the game is placed as the hider by default and sets the hidden combination while giving visual clues to the player by analyzing the combination set by the player/seeker.
  • The A.I. uses a simple search algorithm that goes through the colour combinationatin set by the player.
  • After comparing the player’s combination with its own(The hidden one), the A.I. then provides visual clues in the form of red and white pins(identical to board game).
  • The red pin placed at a particular colour’s position denotes that the colour and the position match the one from hidden combination.
  • The white pin signifies that the colour exists in the hidden combination but at the wrong position.
